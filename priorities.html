Parking at Lakewood Station

The Sound Transit 2 Plan (approved by voters in 2008) envisioned adding new parking stalls at Lakewood Station, to meet expected demand. While the current 600-stall parking garage was nearly filled to capacity most weekdays in 2019, today it is only about 26% full on average, likely due to work-from-home and hybrid schedules.
